Kurapika

    Kurapika (クラピカ, Kurapika) is the last survivor of the Kurta Clan. He is a Blacklist Hunter and the current leader of the organization founded by Light Nostrade. He is a member of the Zodiacs with the codename "Rat" (子ね, Ne). His goal is to avenge his clan and recover the remaining Scarlet Eyes.

  • Blacklist Hunter:
    • (賞金首ブラックリストハンター, Burakkurisuto Hantā—base kanji lit. meaning "Bounty Hunter" as also called in the Viz translation) is a type of Hunter whose skills are dedicated to tracking down dangerous and wanted criminals.

  • Scarlet Eyes:
    • The Scarlet Eyes or Crimson Eyes (緋ひの眼め[2] or 緋ひの目め[3], Hi no Me) are a trait exclusive to members of the Kurta Clan. Particularly when treated as items, they are also known as Kurta Eyeballs (クルタ族ぞくの眼がん球きゅう, Kuruta-zoku no Gankyū). Excluding Kurapika's, only 36 pairs exist. Every secound they are used, an hour get cuts off their lifespan.
