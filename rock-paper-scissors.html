    Rock paper Scissors GUI

    By Grace D. Yeend

    Video of Rock Paper Scissors

    3A

    The overall purpose of the program is to play online rock paper scissors against an AI. The user clicks on one of the three buttons, (rock, paper or scissors) then the computer will return with either rock paper scissors and the screen will display who won. In this case, it was a tie with the user and computer. The input of the code is when the user chooses either rock, paper or scissors. The output is what the computer gives, rock, paper or scissors and the displayed winner.
