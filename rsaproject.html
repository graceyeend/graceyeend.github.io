    The overall purpose of the RSA Project was to help people encrypt and decrypt special messages to each other. Displayed in the video above, on the left you input your public key, n and e than your message in the boxes provided. After the message is written in the thrid box, you press encrypt and it gives you your encrypted message. Bringing the encrypted message to the box to the right, you also input an n and d value or a private key than press decrypt, now you have your message back!
